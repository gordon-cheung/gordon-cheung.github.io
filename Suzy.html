Bae Su-ji

Biography

  • Bae Su-ji (Suzy)
  • Singer, actress, model
  • October 10, 1994

Bae Su-ji, more commonly known as Suzy, is a South Korean singer and actress. She is associated with the South Korean girl group Miss A. Since debuting as a member of Miss A, Suzy has acted in the many dramas including music teen drama Dream High, Uncontrollably Fond, and Gu Family Book.
