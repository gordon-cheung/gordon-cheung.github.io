Biography

  • Lee Ji-eun (IU)
  • Singer-songwriter, actress
  • May 16, 1993

Lee Ji-eun, professionally known as IU is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress. Since her debut in 2008, IU has received over 180 nominations, winning over 70 of them. IU's most recent album is Palette (2017). She has starred in many films such including Dream high, Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo and The Producers.
